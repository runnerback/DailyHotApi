# Prompt: Perler Beads Finished Product Photo

## English Prompt

```
A top-down overhead photograph of a completed Perler bead (fuse bead) artwork placed on a wooden desk, shot with an iPhone.

The Perler bead artwork:
- Based on the provided pattern: [PATTERN]
- Assembled on a 52 x 52 peg pegboard (the pegboard may be partially visible beneath/around the beads)
- Beads are fully fused with a flat, slightly glossy surface showing the characteristic melted-together look of ironed Perler beads
- Each bead retains its round shape with a visible center hole, colors are solid and match standard Perler bead palette

Scene and environment:
- The finished bead artwork is the hero subject, placed slightly off-center on a natural wood-grain desk or tabletop
- Scattered around the artwork: a few loose individual Perler beads in assorted colors, a bead pen (tweezers-style bead placement tool), and optionally a small pile of unsorted beads nearby
- The scene should look casually arranged — not perfectly styled, as if someone just finished crafting and took a quick photo
- Soft, natural indoor lighting (daylight from a window), gentle shadows, no harsh studio flash

Camera and realism:
- Shot from directly above (bird's-eye / flat-lay perspective), with the bead artwork as the dominant element occupying roughly 60-70% of the frame
- Shallow depth-of-field: the bead artwork is tack-sharp, peripheral objects (loose beads, tools) have very slight natural softness
- iPhone camera characteristics: natural color rendering, subtle lens vignette, minor noise in shadow areas, no over-sharpening
- No text, watermarks, or UI overlays
- The image must look like an authentic, unedited casual photo — avoid hyper-clean CGI aesthetics, perfect symmetry, or overly dramatic lighting

Image size: 1024 x 1024 pixels
```

### Negative Prompt (optional)

```
CGI render, 3D render, illustration, cartoon, digital art, studio lighting, perfect symmetry, watermark, text overlay
```

## 中文说明

> 用途：根据拼豆图纸，生成拼豆完成后的真实感实物照片。
> 目标：看起来像 iPhone 随手拍的真实照片，不能一眼看出是 AI 生成。

### 使用方法

1. 将 `[PATTERN]` 替换为具体内容，例如：
   - `a pixel-art panda face in black and white with green bamboo accents`
   - `the provided perler bead pattern image (see attached)`
   - 如果图生图模型支持图片输入，可直接附上拼豆图纸作为参考
2. 如果生成结果 AI 感过强，添加上方的 Negative Prompt

### Prompt 要点

| 要素 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 拼豆作品 | 52x52 板子，珠子已熨烫融合，表面微光泽，中心孔可见 |
| 桌面场景 | 木纹桌面，散落彩色豆子、拼豆笔等工具 |
| 摆放风格 | 随意自然，非完美摆拍 — 像刚做完随手拍 |
| 光照 | 室内自然窗光，柔和阴影，无闪光灯 |
| 构图 | 俯视鸟瞰，作品占画面 60-70%，略偏离中心 |
| 景深 | 作品锐利清晰，周边道具轻微虚化 |
| iPhone 特征 | 自然色彩、轻微暗角、阴影区域有细微噪点、无过度锐化 |
| 尺寸 | 1024 x 1024 像素 |
